Electrical Services

A complete range of residential and commercial electrical services, delivered safely and in full compliance with current electrical codes.

Troubleshooting & Repairs

Find and fix issues fast.

Tripping breakers, dead outlets, flickering lights, or partial power loss — we diagnose the problem, explain it in plain English, and repair it safely.

Panel & Service Upgrades

More power, more safety.

Lighting & Design

Backup & Generators
